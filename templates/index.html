🌱 Origin Story — Why We Built SwasthyaMitra

"It started with a phone call from my mother."

She had just returned from a routine checkup. The doctor handed her a blood report — two pages of numbers, abbreviations, and medical terms. She called me, worried, reading out values she didn't understand. I'm not a doctor. I spent an hour googling each term, still unsure if I was interpreting it right.

That moment of helplessness — felt by millions of families across India every single day — became the seed of SwasthyaMitra.

We built this so that no one has to feel lost holding their own health report. Your body's story deserves to be told in a language you understand.

— The SwasthyaMitra Team

🔭 Our Vision

We envision a future where every Indian — regardless of education, language, or location — can understand their own health without confusion or fear.

Today, medical literacy is a privilege. We're making it a right.

SwasthyaMitra is our first step toward a world where patients walk into doctor's appointments already informed, where rural families don't misread critical reports, and where AI becomes the bridge between medicine and people.

Health clarity for 1.4 billion. That's our mission.

~TIRTH SHAH
